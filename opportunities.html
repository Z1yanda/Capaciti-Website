Advanced Technical Academy

Be guided into career and skill-appropriate paths that encompass DevOps, Java, and Data Analytics

Registration for this programme closes September 2024.

About the programme

This Advanced Technical Academy is a year-long programme aimed at equipping young tech professionals with the skills required to succeed in the digital economy. It begins with 6 months of intensive and immersive training in our Braamfontein Accelerator, where candidates are streamed into career-specific tracks such as DevOps, Java, and Data Analytics. Following this, candidates will participate in a 6-month internship with a CAPACITI corporate partner, allowing them to apply the skills they developed during their training.

Start your learning

  • DevOps
  • Java
  • Data Analytics

Programme Start Date: 01/09/2024

Delivery Modality

Campus based experiential learning and training as workplace simulation. Candidates collaborate and complete workplace activities like problem solving, business communication and complete and submit practical assessments, projects and examination of work undertaken. The training element of the course is delivered over a minimum period of 6 months, supported by extensive job and career critical skills training.

Academic and Qualification Requirements

  • NQF Level 6 or higher qualification in an IT specialised stream
  • Completed qualifications in Computer Science, Information Technology, Data Analytics, Cyber Security and other tech domain specific qualifications
  • Be able to provide your certificate of highest academic achievement

Personal and Eligibility Criteria

  • Have no criminal record and a clean credit history
  • South African nationality
  • Be under or unemployed at the time of application
  • Able to attend full-time training Monday to Friday
  • Able to commit to the full 12-month programme and committed to completing it
  • Be available from September 2024

Commitment and Availability Conditions

  • Able to attend full-time training Monday to Friday
  • Able to commit to the full 12-month programme and committed to completing it
  • Be available from September 2024
  • Dedicated, committed, and willingness to learn

Programme details

Programme Outcomes

  • Onboarding into the workplace for successful candidates upon meeting all training criteria
  • Exposure to real-world applications of technologies and understanding of organisational operations across various technical domains
  • Access to a broad network of field specialists and tech thought leaders
  • Receipt of certifications relevant to their technical stream for career advancement in the digital economy
  • Development of core job skills, self-regulation, financial literacy, and one-to-one mentorship from team members

Job Critical Skills

  • Project based
  • Addresses critical skills gaps
  • Authentic application
  • Guided facilitation and learning
  • Self-aware
  • Confident Communicators
  • Goal Focused
  • Workplace ready

Career Critical

  • Business communications & presentations
  • Investigation, curiosity & creativity
  • Continuous learning
  • Problem solving

Women in Business

Top Technology Tools for Women in Business is a flagship programme aimed specifically at empowering female entrepreneurs to compete effectively in the digital economy.

Registration for this programme is ongoing, register to join our waitlist.

About the programme

The programme offers comprehensive assistance to female entrepreneurs, enhancing their capabilities and delivering strategic guidance.

  • Experiential Learning - Hands-on experience with tech tools
  • Promotes business growth and development
  • Tech Tools and Resources - Introduction to online and digital programs
  • Supports various business activities
  • Holistic Approach - Includes workshops, mentoring, peer learning
  • Focus on soft skills and networking
  • Technology Strategy - Equips women-owned businesses
  • Developing a robust technology strategy

Start your learning

  • Interactive Theory-Based Workshops
  • Mentorship
  • Ecosystem Access
  • Online Learning Platform

Programme Start Date: 09/04/2024

Delivery Modality

The programme will be conducted primarily online/virtually. Active engagement in the programme is mandatory, and all sessions will be conducted in English. Participants must have access to a device for online workshops via Zoom. Additionally, participants are expected to complete surveys, assessments as needed, and online platform modules.

All workshops will be conducted in English.

Recruitment & Selection Criteria

  • Female founders and CEOs with a desire to introduce or improve technology and increase business acumen skills in their businesses (at least 51% female-owned)
  • Must be engaged in a full-time in business
  • Business must be registered (CIPC) and operational for a minimum of 6-12 months
  • Businesses are required to furnish the following documents: Company Business Documents, a valid BBBEE affidavit, and a certified copy of your Identity document (issued within the last 3 months)
  • Applicants must be 21 years or older
  • Be available to join one online workshop per week (2 hours) for the duration of the 10-week programme
  • On completion of the 10-week modular online programme, be available once a month for online group coaching sessions over a period of 10 months
  • Where possible, workshops are held at both our Salt River and Khayelitsha, Braamfontein venues
  • An online platform will be utilised to deliver components of the programme. If face-to-face workshops are required, successful applicants must be able to travel to one of the specified venues

Sector Requirements

Businesses from various sectors are encouraged to apply, including:

  • Automotive services (e.g., auto body repairers, motor traders)
  • Construction and building (e.g., building contractors, carpet and flooring)
  • Claims management
  • Security and electronics (e.g., electronic security)
  • Miscellaneous areas such as audiovisual and solar heating sales & installations

Business Criteria

  • Business must be registered (CIPC) and operational for a minimum of 6-12 months
  • Must not be part of any other incubation / acceleration programme
  • Own device, data, and a reliable/stable internet connection
  • Comfortable with remote teamwork, collaboration and using digital tools

Programme details

Theory Based Workshops

Hybrid, interactive theory-based workshops covering key entrepreneurial concepts such as Digital Marketing.

Mentorship

Combination of group coaching sessions as well as one-on-one sessions with a dedicated mentor.

Online Learning Platform

Access to an online learning platform, with continuous access to personalised resources for knowledge enhancement.

Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP)

Join our inaugural cohort in Cape Town, South Africa.

Registration for this programme opens in June 2025

About the programme

Our one-week Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) Intensive Training Programme provides a thorough introduction to GMP principles, focusing on manufacturing, quality assurance, and regulatory compliance within the food and pharmaceutical industries. This programme combines theoretical insights with practical, hands-on experiences, ensuring participants gain a deep understanding of GMP standards. Over five days, you'll explore critical aspects such as facility management, process control, cleaning and validation, and preparation for regulatory inspections. By the end of the course, you'll be equipped with the knowledge and skills to ensure compliance, improve product quality, and meet regulatory requirements in the manufacturing environment.

Start Learning

  • Understand and implement GMP requirements in food and pharmaceutical settings
  • Design, manage, and maintain compliant facilities
  • Apply best practices in cleaning, validation, and process control
  • Recognise and prepare for regulatory agency inspections
  • Identify differences between GMP requirements for various product types, including biological and botanical products
  • Analyse case studies to apply GMP principles in real-world scenarios

Programme Start Date: 29/09/2025

Delivery Modality

In-person lectures and hands-on laboratory experience. All activities will be conducted in Cape Town, South Africa. Active engagement in the programme is mandatory, and all sessions will be conducted in English.

Academic and Qualification Requirements

  • Current BSc, BTech chemistry students or biotechnology students with strong knowledge of chemistry
  • Maximum education level NQF 8
  • Chemistry and analytical chemistry background
  • Be able to provide your certificate of highest academic achievement

Personal & Legal Eligibility Criteria

  • Have no criminal record and a clean credit history
  • Rwandan national or permitted to reside in South Africa
  • Between the age range of 18 to 35 years old
  • Be under or unemployed at the time of application

Commitment and Availability Conditions

  • Being able to attend full-time course in Cape Town– NO relocation costs will be covered
  • Able to attend full-time training Monday toFriday
  • Able to commit to the full month of training
  • Be available to start from September 2025

Programme details

Programme Highlights

  • Interactive Learning: Real-world scenarios and hands-on exercises
  • Industry-Specific Insights: Tailored discussions on both food and pharma GMPs
  • Specialized Focus Areas: Dedicated sessions on advanced topics, including biological products and aseptic processing
  • Certification of Completion: A certificate awarded upon successful completion of the course, adding to participants' professional credentials

Key Learning Benefits

  • Expert Guidance: Learn from seasoned instructors with industry experience
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with other professionals in the industry
  • Practical Applications: Gain knowledge that is directly applicable to the workplace

Key Objectives

  • Understand and implement GMP requirements in food and pharmaceutical settings
  • Design, manage, and maintain compliant facilities
  • Apply best practices in cleaning, validation, and process control
  • Recognise and prepare for regulatory agency inspections
  • Identify differences between GMP requirements for various product types, including biological and botanical products
  • Analyse case studies to apply GMP principles in real-world scenarios

Frequently Asked Questions

How and when will a successful candidate be notified?

The successful candidates will receive notification via email, please ensure you check your spam folder regularly. This communication is anticipated to take place in the new year, ensuring a comprehensive and timely update on the results.

What are the steps during the application process?

Our application process consists of several stages. First, candidates will be provided with a link to an online test to evaluate their cognitive abilities. Successful candidates will then undergo a phone interview. The final stage involves an in-person interview.

Is there a stipend for the programme?

Yes, all of the interventions offered by UVU Bio are funded and stipends are provided to all candidates. We do not sponsor any relocation costs.

Can I do this programme online?

Our programmes are not virtual; they offer a face to face experience at our lab and training facility, aligning closely with the real-world work environment and exposure to laboratory equipment.
